Quick Start

When you initially install Net Deck, you will need to at least download the card data from netrunnerdb.com. You can optionally also download all available images in advance, useful when you plan on using the app while offline or on a slow GSM/Edge connection. Missing images will be automatically downloaded when needed.

To import decks from other sources, there are three ways:

  • Connect the app to your Dropbox account, copy the Deck files (.o8d) to the “Apps/Net Deck” folder of your Dropbox, then tap on “Import Decks”.
  • Copy a deck list from Safari, Mail etc to your clipboard. On the next app start, you will be asked if you want to import that deck.
  • Copy a netrunnerdb.com decklist URL to your clipboard. On the next app start, you will be asked if you want to download and import that deck.
